Fazenda Corrego Alegre

Half of this property is pristine virgin Atlantic Rainforest dating back long before the last ice age. It is one of the largest remaining fragments of this most devastated Brazilian biome of which 93% has been destroyed and converted to towns, cities and large scale monoculture agriculture with intensive use of toxic defensive agro-chemicals. It is extremely rare to find a fragment of Atlantic Rainforest above 500 hectares. In fact less than 10% of the existing remaining original biome is 500 hectares or larger. In other words less than 0.7% of the original biome is in a contiguous block of 500 hectares or more. Hence an untouched area of 2,000 hectares is extremely rare, valuable and of vital importance to mankind and the planet.
